Multi-Cultural · Multi-Lingual · Multi-Cuisine

Singapore has never fallen short of being the top 5 richest countries in the world in recent years.

The festivities, traditional building, and multicultural food that we celebrate have brought vibrancy to the fabric of our Study Tour Program. The diverse cultures have made us more knowledgeable and offered us more avenues to cultivate the arts.

The Immersive Tour

 

Our Singapore Study Tour program is a fusion of classroom learning and a myriad of outdoor activities, such as trans-island tour, hiking in the forest, museums and galleries visits, partying, barbeque on the beach, and sports. Sign-up to join your favourite activities suiting your own schedule.

 

In the various activities organized by the study tour, you will use the newly learned language skills, taste the various delicious ethnic cuisines, explore the old colonial area and go shopping all the way until you arrive at Marina Bay, or relax on the white sand beach on the island.

 

We not only let you explore Singapore's culture and scenery, but also effectively improve your English and Chinese. It is your excellent bilingual study tour.
