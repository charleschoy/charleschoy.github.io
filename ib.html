The International Baccalaureate (IB) School Programme is a challenging two-year long curriculum designed for students between 13 to 19 years of age. As an internationally recognised qualification that is usable for many of the world’s leading universities, this IB Chinese course will equip you with the expertise and qualifications you need to apply for the job position you’ve always wanted.

Anytime Mandarin offers IB Chinese course to cater to international school students looking to pick up and become proficient in the language. We have an established track record of assisting our students in achieving excellent results in their Chinese IB examinations.

Our Chinese teachers assesses students on their Chinese language listening, speaking, reading and writing skills of the course and identify the areas that the student needs to improve. We customise the Chinese course content accordingly to meet the specific needs of the student. Whether students are looking for overall improvement in language proficiency level, or guidance and home tuition for their school syllabus, we have solutions to help them achieve their goals.

Our International Baccalaureate (IB) Chinese Course will impart the necessary knowledge for you not only to read, write but also speak proficiently in Mandarin. We also prepare you for the IB examination itself through the assigning of past examination papers for practice.
