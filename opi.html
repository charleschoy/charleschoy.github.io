​Our OPI (Oral Proficiency Interview) Prep Course is tailored to help individuals prepare for the OPI, which assesses a person's speaking and communication skills in a foreign language. The OPI is commonly required for US government and corporate international roles where language proficiency is essential. Here's what you can expect from our OPI Prep Course:

• Oral Proficiency Training: Our OPI Prep Course focuses on developing verbal communication skills. You'll engage in speaking exercises, conversations, and simulated interview scenarios to enhance your ability to communicate effectively in Mandarin.

• Targeted Exam Strategies: We provide strategies and techniques specific to the OPI format. Our experienced instructors help you understand the structure of the OPI and teach you how to present your Mandarin language skills during the interview.

• Mock Interviews: Practical experience is vital. We conduct mock OPI interviews that closely resemble the actual exam. It helps you become familiar with the interview process, build confidence, and receive constructive feedback.

• Individualized Feedback: We offer individualized feedback based on your performance in mock interviews. This feedback allows you to identify your strengths and areas that require improvement.

• Customized Study Plans: Every student or domain has different language proficiency levels and goals. We work with you to create a study plan that suits your needs, be it achieving a particular OPI level or improving your overall language proficiency.

• Experienced Instructors: Our instructors are experienced in Mandarin teaching as a foreign language and thoroughly understand OPI requirements. They guide you throughout the course and provide valuable insights.

• Flexible Learning Options: We offer various learning formats, including in-person classes, online courses, and self-paced study, so you can choose the method that suits your learning style and schedule.

• Progress Tracking: Regular assessments and progress tracking ensure that you're on the right track to achieving your desired OPI score or proficiency level.

Our OPI Prep Course is suitable for government employees, diplomats, international business professionals, and anyone else required to take the OPI. It's designed to help you reach your Mandarin Chinese proficiency level and perform well during the OPI interview. We're committed to supporting your language learning and exam preparation needs.

All instruction is in small groups with a native Mandarin speaking instructor who is familiar with the needs of US Government OSI. You'll meet your tutor, complete an initial assessment, and receive a custom study plan adapted to your needs throughout the course.
